CSV | Excel to HTML Tableizer

[Click for instructions.]
  1. Copy and Paste CSV or Excel/TSV data into the ENTER DATA HERE box.
  2. Hit GO!
  3. Apply formatting:
    • CELLS -- Click color swatch or attribute swatch then click individual cell to apply. Dbl click to reset formatting on an individual cell.
    • ROWS -- Drag swatch onto row to apply swatch to the entire row.
    • COLUMNS -- Drag the swatch to the column header to apply to column header.
  4. Hit HTML! Copy and paste HTML into your blog or page.
